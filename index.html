Notary public

We have been providing notarial services for over 20 years.

A notary public witnesses signatures on legal documents, takes sworn documents, administers oaths and certifies the authenticity of legal documents for use overseas. A notary public must have practised law for more than ten years, owned or been a partner of a law firm for over seven years and been appointed by the Archbishop of Canterbury in England.
